About Our Dog Feeding Reminder

Our goal is to help pet owners never forget to feed their dogs on time! This simple tool sends a reminder after you’ve fed your dogs, so you can stay on top of their meals.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide an easy and effective way for dog owners to keep track of feeding times, ensuring their furry friends are always well-fed and happy!

How to Use

Simply click the "I've fed my dogs" button on the home page when you feed them. The website will automatically set a reminder to notify you when it's time for the next feeding.

Questions and Concerns

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to us through our contact form on the website. We're here to help!
